Jon Hurwitz | Hayden Schlossberg
Stars:
Jason Biggs, Alyson Hannigan, Seann William Scott, Chris Klein
Watch Trailer

The Buzz: Looking at the poster for the first movie, we're feeling a bit sentimental: Tara Reid was untouched by cosmetic surgery, Natasha Lyonne was luminescent, and Chris Klein was an emerging comic actor (he was perfectly cast in Election (1999)). Kudos to Universal relegating the spin-offs to the world of direct-to-DVD entertainment, though we imagine a full-on reboot will come next. And with the guys who made Harold & Kumar Go to White Castle (2004) in the director's chairs, this could be the filthiest chapter yet?

Damsels in Distress (2011)

99 min   -   Comedy | Drama | Romance

Metascore: 67/100 (33 reviews)

Director:
Whit Stillman
Stars:
Greta Gerwig, Adam Brody, Analeigh Tipton, Megalyn Echikunwoke
Watch Trailer

The Buzz: Metropolitan (1990) and Barcelona (1994) were the films some of us quoted throughout college as we fumbled our way through those pretentious years, unaware that Whit Stillman would make his best film at the end of the 1990s, The Last Days of Disco (1998), which was criminally mishandled as a theatrical release. Stillman seemingly vanished during the following decade, and I think the story on his whereabouts would make for a great memoir, but in the meantime, we have a new film to celebrate - one that coaxes out Greta Gerwig's best performance to date, surprises with an array of musical numbers, and keeps the humor going through the end credits.

Watch Trailer
