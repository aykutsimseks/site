129 min   -   Drama | Romance

Director:

Stars:

Watch Trailer

The Buzz: For all of its adaptations, Emily Brontë's 1847 novel has never been more in-demand, with this project seducing director Andrea Arnold (who scrapped an existing screenplay and drafted her own for her follow-up to Fish Tank (2009)), and NBC's recent announcement that a Napa-set version is in development for the small screen. Cloud Atlas (2012)

172 min   -   Drama | Sci-Fi

Metascore: 55/100 (45 reviews)
An exploration of how the actions of individual lives impact one another in the past, present and future, as one soul is shaped from a killer into a hero, and an act of kindness ripples across centuries to inspire a revolution.
Directors:
Tom Tykwer | Andy Wachowski | Lana Wachowski
Stars:
Tom Hanks, Halle Berry, Hugh Grant, Hugo Weaving
Watch Trailer

The Buzz: Hugh Grant plays a killer, Susan Sarandon plays a man, and Tom Hanks makes his most engaging starring-role choice in years in the new project from the Wachowski Starship, Andy and Lana's collaboration with co-director Tom Twyker ,and an adaptation of David Mitchell's celebrated science-fiction novel that sounds like the literary offspring of Ray Bradbury and Samuel Delany. Reincarnation is a main thread, and we're stoked for the visuals and nervous about narrative cohesion or lack thereof. In a year where Prometheus (2012) proved that a film can be a spectacle, an epic, and somewhat of a letdown all at once, the Wachowskis and company have tricky territory to navigate if they want both box office and critical success -- and there better not be any shenanigans (or dance-rave numbers) a la The Matrix Revolutions (2003). Also, few things this year are as watchable as Lana's recent speech, where she accepted the Human Rights Campaign's Visibility Award.
